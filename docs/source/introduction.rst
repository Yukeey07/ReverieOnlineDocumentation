Introduction to Reverie Online
==============================

**Reverie Online** is a massively multiplayer online action role-playing game (MMOARPG) that combines the charm of pixel art with fast-paced multiplayer gameplay. Players enter a vast world full of dungeons, quests, and secrets, where they can fight enemies, gather resources, craft items, and explore procedurally generated environments.

What is Reverie Online?
-----------------------

Reverie Online offers a seamless multiplayer experience where players can collaborate in real-time to explore an expansive world filled with combat challenges, crafting opportunities, and dynamic events.
